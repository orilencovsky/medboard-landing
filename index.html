THE SCIENCE

Learning backed by how the brain actually works

MeduXa is built on nine evidence-based learning principles — with an AI layer that adapts to each student.

01

Active Recall

Learning is driven by retrieving answers, not re-reading them. Every session forces retrieval.

02

Spaced Repetition

Review is scheduled to fight the forgetting curve — questions resurface exactly when memory fades.

03

Interleaved Practice

Topics are mixed, not blocked. Switching between subjects forces your brain to discriminate — the way the real exam demands.

04

Targeted Practice

Study time flows to your weakest topics first — never spread evenly across material you've already mastered.

05

Error-Driven Remediation

The questions you missed — and the ones you were sure about but got wrong — come back as a focused deck to drill.

06

Self-Explanation

Before the answer unlocks, you explain your reasoning in your own words — the single act that deepens understanding most.

07

Confidence Calibration

Rate how sure you are before each answer. The app surfaces where you're confident but wrong — the most dangerous gap.

08

Guided Discovery

The tutor draws the answer out of you with questions instead of handing it over — the effort of reasoning is what makes it stick.

09

Source-Grounded Truth

Every explanation is backed by a verbatim citation from the textbook. Never a hallucination.
