The Science

Learning backed by how the brain actually works

MedBoard IL is built on five evidence-based principles — not just a question bank with a chatbot attached.

🔁
Active Recall

Learning is driven by retrieving answers, not re-reading them. Every session forces retrieval.

📅
Spaced Repetition

Review is scheduled to fight the forgetting curve — questions resurface exactly when memory fades.

🗺️
Targeted Practice

A heat map exposes your weak topics so every study minute goes where it matters most.

🤖
Socratic AI Tutoring

A per-question AI tutor that asks before it tells — adapting to how you think, not just what you got wrong.

📖
Source-Grounded Truth

Every explanation is backed by a verbatim citation from the textbook. Never a hallucination.
